### THE PROBLEM STATEMENT

In September 2024, the mountains around Asheville and WNC came down. Thousands of landslides. 31 lives lost. When recovery began, one question stopped everything: where is it safe to rebuild?
The demand for expert hazard assessments has overwhelmed the capacity of the few specialists who can answer that question. Meanwhile, the companies already using this data are using it to protect their shareholders — not the homeowners living on the hillside.
SafeTerrainIQ changes that — putting expert-level geohazard intelligence directly in the hands of homeowners, engineers, and communities.

Expert-level geohazard assessments take weeks to months, cost tens of thousands of dollars, and require specialists that most developers, homeowners, and municipalities cannot access. No platform currently exists that delivers this directly to the people who need it most.


### THE SOLUTION

Search for any property, get a hazard and risk assessment in the same day — powered by real numerical modeling and an agentic AI trained by landslide experts that learns YOUR risk profile and tailors results to YOU and your home.
The dirt delivered right to your dashboard.

### User Flow Of App

- We then prompt the user with a series of questions. These questions can be clickable, multiple choice, sliders, buttons, etc.
